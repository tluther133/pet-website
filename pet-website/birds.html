About Birds

Birds come in different shapes and sizes, such as:

  • Parrots
  • Finches
  • Doves
  • Pigeons

Feeding

To know what birds eat, obsreve the nature of their beak. The birds listed above can be fed with seeds of different sizes.Pay attention to their beak size to avoid choking.They can be also given bird feed. And don't forget water!

Grooming

Generally, it is advisable to have a bird bath outside(Ensure it is high so as to prevent other animals from drinking!). And change the water daily to avoid health risks for your pet.

Housing

Bird houses vary from a birdhouse to a cage. Take the bird's physical size into account, as you would want to fit their food and water sources in an easily accesiable area.
