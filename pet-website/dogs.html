About Dogs

Dogs give you attention, provided you give them something to eat and drink!:

Feeding

Dogs are carnivorous, so give them enough quantites of meat, ensure it is properly washed.

Grooming

Give your dogs a bath daily, trim their nails and give them a shave. Remember to take them to the vet occasionally.

Housing

Dogs need a place to sleep, if having a doghouse is too much. Ensure it is clean at all times, and is properly trained.
