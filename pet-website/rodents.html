About Rodents

Rodents are more demanding than one may think, given their solitary nature. They have multiple needs that have to be accounted for.

Common Rodent Pets

  • Guinea Pigs
  • Hamsters
  • Mice

Feeding

Rodents generally eat the same things e.g vegetables, fruits and some nuts. Water helps in digestion.

Grooming

Trim your pet's hair and nails occasionally, to prevent them from getting lice or infections.

Housing

Rodents do not require large areas, they can even be put in your breast pocket Just have enough food and water, and they will be alright.
